How to Use

Hair Oil

  • Warm a few drops in your palms.
  • Massage gently into scalp and roots.
  • Leave overnight or at least 30 minutes before rinsing.
  • Use 2–3 times a week for best results.

Lipstick

  • Start with clean, moisturized lips.
  • Apply directly or use a brush for precision.
  • Blot lightly and layer for deeper color.
  • Reapply after meals or as desired.

Lip Balm

  • Apply a thin layer on dry or cracked lips.
  • Reapply throughout the day as needed.
  • Use before bedtime for overnight healing.
  • Can be used as a base before applying lipstick.
